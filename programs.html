PILLAR 01

Music Access.

Low-barrier, community-centered music learning — designed to remove the intimidation that often surrounds music education.

Wilmington has a working musical culture. What's missing is the rung that turns audience members into players — and the dignified paid teaching work that local musicians deserve.

Every session is paid teaching for a local musician. Every participant pays what they can.

  • Beginner workshops
  • Open jam sessions
  • Youth rhythm & percussion circles
  • Singer-songwriter circles
  • Local musician demonstrations
  • "First Instrument" nights
  • DJ & beat-making basics
  • Recording fundamentals

PILLAR 02

Painting &
Visual Arts.

The easiest pillar to launch first. Supplies are manageable, sessions are flexible, and the outcomes are immediately visible on the walls of the space.

From a first community paint night to a "Paint Your Story" workshop that turns cultural identity into a finished piece — visual arts is how Luna gets known.

Family days are designed for kids and parents to make side by side. Murals leave permanent marks on the neighborhood.

  • Community paint nights
  • Youth painting classes
  • Family art days
  • Beginner acrylic workshops
  • Mural design sessions
  • "Paint Your Story"
  • Local artist-led classes
  • Watercolor fundamentals

PILLAR 03

Rotating Art Method.
A new discipline every month.

The Rotating Method keeps the space fresh, broadens the audience, and gives many local creatives the chance to teach. Each month is built around a single guest discipline.

Photography
Phones welcome. Composition, light, and storytelling with whatever you carry.
Open studio

Collage

All ages

Showcase

Printmaking
Linocuts and gelli plates. Multiples. Tangible craft.

How it costs

Donation-based access
is the whole point.

Nobody is excluded because they cannot pay. Our suggested giving levels are a guide, not a gate. Pay-what-you-can is real here.

If you can pay above the suggested amount, you're paying for someone else who can't. That's the model.

Sustain a class with a recurring gift
FreeIf money is the barrier today
$5 — $10Pay-what-you-can floor
$15 — $25Suggested for most workshops
$50+Sustaining gift — covers a free seat
