Getting here.

Free street parking after 6 PM and on weekends. The Whale Building corridor sits a short walk from the riverfront and just up from the Wilmington train station.

Bike racks on the front entry. The corridor is well-lit through 11 PM Friday and Saturday.

Accessibility.

Ground-floor entry, ADA-accessible restrooms, and a quiet room available on request. If you have specific access needs — mobility, sensory, language — email hello@lunawilm.org ahead of your visit and we'll make sure the room is ready.

Spanish-language programming is on the calendar. Bilingual instructors are noted on each class page.

Press & media

Writing about Luna?

We love a thoughtful story — about the founding, the corridor, the artists, or the question of who gets to make art in Wilmington. Founder interviews and tours are available by request.
